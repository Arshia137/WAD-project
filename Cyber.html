Course Description

Get started on your journey to becoming a cybersecurity expert with our comprehensive Introduction to Cyber Security course. This course covers everything from the basics of cyber security, including key concepts and popular attacks, to advanced topics such as cryptography, security goals and their implementations, and designing security systems. You will also learn about vulnerabilities such as buffer overflow and participate in a case study on a real-world WhatsApp attack. This course is designed to be hands-on and engaging, with practical projects, case studies, and demonstrations to help you master the skills you need to succeed in the field of cybersecurity.
